Bailey

Labrador Retriever

Bailey is a beautiful Labrador Retriever with a sleek short-haired coat. She is a bundle of energy and loves nothing more than playing fetch in the park. Despite her high energy levels, Bailey is incredibly affectionate and adores cuddling up with her humans after a long day of play. She gets along well with other dogs and is very friendly with children, making her an ideal family pet. Bailey is two years old and weighs approximately 60 pounds. Her adoption fee is $200.
